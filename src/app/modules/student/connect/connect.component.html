Dear Students,
Among the oldest and largest university in India, KEMU was established in 1860. It is a training center where great advances are being made in both teaching and patient care, yet never leaving behind the age old ethical and moral principles which embody the essence of any studet. Also called the Harvard of East, KEMU chairs the credit for innumerable professionally outstanding and morally sound doctors. They have been serving humanity in all countries in all ages and all levels of society. The major functions of KEMU are not sporadic events but on going story of tradition itself. We, The KAPS (Kemcolians’ Arts and Photography Society) under the great leadership and guidance of Prof Dr. Aftab Asif, have a major role in not only keeping these traditions alive but are also setting new trends for the others to follow. We have been holding Annual Arts and Photography Exhibition at the National Level for the past 9 years and the response of the various Academic schools, Colleges and Universities have been massive in this regard. To keep the tradition alive, we are pleased to announce the Kemcolians’ 13th Annual Inter-collegiate Arts & Photography Exhibition on April 17, 2019.
